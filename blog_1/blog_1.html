Who is BFC?

Since 2015, Brooklyn Film Camera has become a shop and community space that meets the needs of photographers at both professional and amateur levels alike. Their space is home to the NYC film photography community

Services

They provide:Polaroid Restoration, Film Camera Repairs, Film Processing, PhotoBooth Services They also, Host reguarly workshops, classes, critiques, screenings, gatherings, events, and more

Something Unique

Gallery : Brooklyn Film Camera's gallery is the core of their mission as a community-focused space. Exhibitions rotate monthly and feature artists both local and distant who are pushing the medium of film photography in interesting and innovative ways. And they do ship worldwide!
