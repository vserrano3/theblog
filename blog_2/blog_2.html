Who is K & M Cameras?

K&M Camera opened its first store over 40 years ago, in 1976. Their goals are to make sure you can get everything you need to explore your world, capture your moments, and live your life-all at one location.

Services

They offer a wide variety of high high-end photography brands like, Canon, Nikon, Hasselblad, Mamiya, Profoto, Apple. Offering thousands of products for rent, enabling everyone to get the most out of their photo and video shoots.Rentals can be easily made in the store or through their website.

Something Unique

They have such a wide variety of both digital cameras & film cameras. They've been around for a long time have so they loyal customers, and most of their staff are experienced photographers who are more than happy to explain which camera will benefit you in the long run.
